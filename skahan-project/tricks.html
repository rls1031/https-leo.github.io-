I know over 50 tricks and commands!

Aside from my agility commands and tasks, my mom has taught me some tricks just for fun as well!

  • Sneeze and wipe nose
  • Stomp feet
  • Smile
  • Jump into mom's arms
  • Spin

  • sit pretty
  • Put toys away
  • Balance treat on nose

We're always working on new things too! Right now I'm learning how to stick out my tongue and jump rope! Learning a new trick is kind of like solving a puzzle; what is mom looking for me to do? It keeps my brain busy and engaged!
