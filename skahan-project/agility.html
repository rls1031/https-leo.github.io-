2017 AKC European Open Junior Agility Team USA

In 2017 we competed on the AKC's US junior agility team in Luxembourg with 23 other team members. This was my first time out of the country and we had a great time competing. It was awesome to hang with junior handlers from 50 different countries! This trial was on my third birthday; my mom and I made memories that will last a lifetime!

Agility is dog sport where a dog completes an obstacle course in a race for time and accurracy. Qualifying scores are given to teams in the masters level class for running the course under time and with no mistakes. There are different class levels including novice, open, excellent, and masters. Some of the course styles include standard (all equiptment), jumpers (jump, tunnels, and weave poles), and premier (international style).
